FAQs

Answers to common questions about our ops engineering solutions

What does Panoramica actually do? +

We help businesses replace ad-hoc execution with systems that run consistently.

Our work focuses on how customers are acquired, how work moves through the organisation, and how decisions are made. We take what is currently implicit or fragmented and turn it into clear, documented operating logic that teams can follow without constant supervision.

The outcome is simpler execution, fewer handovers, and better use of time and data across the business.

What do you mean by “designing systems”? +

A system is a defined way of operating that produces the same result without relying on individual judgement.

In practice, this often includes clarifying your ideal customer profile, structuring your channel mix, defining lifecycle stages and decision gates, and setting rules for ownership and handovers. We also decide which metrics actually matter at each stage and design workflows around them.

Instead of people deciding what to do next, the system makes that decision clear.

How do you make this measurable? +

Every engagement starts with a baseline.

Before building anything, we identify the metrics that reflect how the business is really performing. This may include conversion rates between stages, time to response, cycle time per process, or pipeline accuracy. Once systems are implemented, we track changes against those baselines.

The goal is not more dashboards, but fewer surprises and more predictable outcomes.

How fast can we expect to see results? +

Most clients see meaningful improvements within the first 30 days.

Early gains usually come from clearer workflows, reduced manual work, or faster decision-making. Over 8 to 12 weeks, this typically translates into more consistent execution, better visibility across teams, and improved performance against core metrics.

We optimise for progress you can feel quickly, not long transformation programmes.

Who is Panoramica best suited for? +

We primarily work with SaaS and financial services businesses, where complexity, compliance, and scale demand disciplined operations.

That said, we are open to working with adjacent industries facing similar challenges, particularly where teams, tools, and data are no longer aligned. If your business is growing but operational clarity is lagging behind, the work is usually a strong fit.
